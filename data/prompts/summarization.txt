Summarize the following in two or three sentences: Quantization reduces the numerical precision of model weights from 32-bit floats to lower bit-widths such as 8-bit or 4-bit integers. This shrinks memory footprint and speeds up arithmetic on hardware that accelerates integer operations. Post-training quantization applies after training with no retraining required. Quantization-aware training folds the quantization error into the training loop for better accuracy at the same bit-width.
Summarize the following in two or three sentences: Retrieval-augmented generation pairs a retrieval system with a generative model. The retriever selects relevant passages from an external corpus and the generator conditions on both the user query and the retrieved context. This approach reduces hallucination, allows knowledge to be updated without retraining, and makes the knowledge base inspectable. Summarize the following in two or three sentences: Knowledge distillation trains a small student model to mimic the output distributions of a larger teacher model. Rather than training on hard labels, the student learns from the teacher's softened probability vectors, which carry richer supervisory signal. This allows compact models to approach the performance of much larger ones while being cheaper to serve. Distillation is commonly used to create lightweight models suitable for edge deployment.
